Subscribe to Atom feed

Atom is a language similar to RSS. It is used on many webpages. By using a Reader, you can receive a syndication of recent updates from every website you follow. I personally use Feedbro as my Atom and RSS reader; Feedbro is a browser extension. If Feedbro does not appeal, you may want to read Zapier's Top 5 Feed Aggregators.
